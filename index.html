About SLIoT Challenge

What is SLIoT Challenge?

SLIoT Challenge 2019 is a Competition which is organized by Computer Science and Engineering department of University of Moratuwa, and Institute of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL) in collaboration with Sri Lanka Telecom PLC. This competition is organized to improve the awareness on the concept 'Internet of Things' (IoT) and its usages among the Sri Lankan community.
The concept of 'internet of things' is fast evolving, thereby expanding horizons for different industries like automobile, transportation, health-care, and lot more. The market for IoT devices is growing rapidly and the market for IoT will be around $520 billion by 2021. SLIoT challenge aims to provide young entrepreneurs and students a chance to engage with the IoT industry.

The organizers expect to evaluate IoT based solutions which are capable in providing solutions to general public according to Industrial level standards.
By this Competition we hope to ignite imagination and creativity by providing a competitive background among each other.
We will check your ideas in various aspects to determine its creativity, value, benefits, technologies etc. Winners will be chosen from the best ideas and event day performance of the applications.
